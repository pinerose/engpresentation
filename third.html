3. the Protectionism

the Rise of Neo-Mercantilism

After the beginning of industrial capitalism and the first industrial revolution,
the mercantilism was fading away.
However, the emerging industrial country in the second half of 19th century
enforced the mercantilist policies to protect their infant industry.
Because this policy’s purpose is very similar to mercantilism,
this is called “neo-mercantilism” or “protectionism”.

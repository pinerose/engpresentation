1. the Mercantilism

the Virtuous Circle between national wealth and national power

In the textbook, Christopher Columbus tried to find
the route to India to get black pepper.
However, it is well known that Isabel I supported him.
because he just wanted to seize enormous wealth and she knew that,
their is no reason for her to support him.
Until Adam smith wrote the wealth of nations,
the economic policy governing European society was mercantilism.
From a mercantilism point of view, when the country has lots of gold,
there is much currency, and it will be used to build up the national power.
This is utilized to bring lots of gold.
This is the virtuous circle of national wealth and national power.
